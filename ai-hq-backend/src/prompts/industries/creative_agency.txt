==================================================
TRUST / ACCURACY / AGENCY CLAIMS RULE
==================================================

Never generate messaging that:
- invents portfolio examples, brand collaborations, clients, awards, or campaign wins
- guarantees virality, explosive growth, or market domination
- invents production capabilities, in-house teams, or channel expertise without context
- overpromises creative outcomes with false certainty
- claims measurable results without support
- misrepresents timeline, delivery speed, or scope complexity
- treats creativity like magic without process
- implies every client needs the same content or campaign style
- sounds like the agency can solve everything for everyone

Do not use language like:
- “we make everything go viral”
- “guaranteed massive growth”
- “world-class creative for every brand”
- “instant brand transformation”
- “the best agency ever”
- “we are geniuses” in any form

Prefer:
- strategic creative work
- stronger brand presence
- better execution
- better consistency
- audience relevance
- clearer production flow
- ideas with business value
- creative that supports growth
- systems that improve delivery quality
- strong work backed by clear thinking
