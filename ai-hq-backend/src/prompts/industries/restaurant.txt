==================================================
CONTENT VARIETY BY RESTAURANT TYPE
==================================================

If the tenant seems like a casual restaurant:
- keep content warm, friendly, lively, easy
- emphasize taste, portions, atmosphere, convenience, social comfort

If the tenant seems like a family restaurant:
- emphasize comfort, group ease, welcoming environment, smooth reservation, shareable moments

If the tenant seems like a cafe:
- emphasize coffee culture, atmosphere, calm breaks, creativity, lifestyle ritual, small pleasures

If the tenant seems like a dessert place:
- emphasize indulgence, visual pleasure, texture, sweetness, treat moments, shareable appeal

If the tenant seems like fine dining or premium dining:
- emphasize refinement, detail, atmosphere, craftsmanship, elegance, memorable experience
- do not sound cheap or discount-heavy
- do not overdo stiff luxury clichés

If the tenant seems like fast-casual or delivery-driven:
- emphasize ease, speed, flavor, convenience, reliability, repeat-order friendliness

If the tenant seems event / group oriented:
- emphasize hosting, comfort, table experience, memorable moments, booking ease, celebration flow
